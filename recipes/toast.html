Toast

Description

toast is when you cook bread and it becomes crispy and hot. toast is a highly customisable food that can be eaten with just about anything: my favourites include spreads like jam and honey, but you can really get crazy with it.

Ingredients

  • bread

Method

  1. put bread in toaster - one slice per slot
  2. press the button and wait til it pops out
  3. eat it. or add stuff with it and then eat it.
